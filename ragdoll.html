Ragdoll Blending System

A physics-driven character system demonstrating limb-based impacts, ragdoll takeover, and animation recovery.

1. Click-to-Ragdoll with Limb-Specific Impacts

The zombie normally walks toward the camera with AI-driven locomotion. When the player clicks on the character, the system raycasts to find the exact limb hit. A directional force is then applied specifically to that rigidbody — not the root — producing natural, physically accurate reactions.

The character collapses fully into ragdoll mode, letting Unity’s physics simulation take over until recovery.

2. Recovery: Detecting Fall Direction & Standing Up

After a short configurable delay, the system evaluates whether the character has fallen face-up or face-down by checking the ragdoll's hip orientation.

Based on this, it selects and plays the appropriate stand-up animation clip. A smooth blend drives the transition from ragdoll physics back into animator control, allowing the zombie to resume walking and continue chasing the player.
